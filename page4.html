The Philippines is a country located in Asia, specifically in the Southeast. It consists of more than 7,000 islands, making the country an archipelago . The country is known for its diverse geography, which includes mountains, plains, and coastal areas. Here are the top travel spots in the Phillipines.

Boracay

This is one of the best tourist destinations in the Philippines. This gorgeous beach has crystal clear waters and beautiful white sand. Boracay also has 5 star hotels, making the place a famous honeymoon spot.

Bohol

The Chocolate Hills of Bohol are truly a geomorphic wonder. Unfortunately, they aren't made of chocolate. The Chocolate Hills is a must-visit when you go to Bohol.

Ifugao

The Banaue Rice Terraces of Ifugao is a beautifully made structure made by the people of Ifugao 2000 years ago. The emerald green hue of this destination is a must-see when you visit Ifugao.
