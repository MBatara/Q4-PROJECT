CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE

Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin
  • Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin or simply just Crisostomo Ibarra, is a young, wealthy and intelligent filipino man who studied at Europe for over 7 years.
  • He is the son of Don Rafael Ibarra.
    • Don Rafael Ibarra
  • Don Rafael Ibarra was one of the most richest men in San Diego. He was also generous and intelligent like his son.
  • He is the father of Crisostomo Ibarra.
    • Maria Clara
  • Maria Clara, is an intelligent and beautiful filipino woman who also studied in Europe.
  • She is the adoptive daughter of Kapitan Tiago, and she is the biological daughter of Padre Damaso.
    • Kapitan Tiago
  • Kapitan Tiago is a very intelligent and wealthy business man.
  • He is the adoptive father of Maria Clara.
    • Padre Damaso
  • Padre Damaso is a very corrupt and mean priest.
  • He is the biological father of Maria Clara.
